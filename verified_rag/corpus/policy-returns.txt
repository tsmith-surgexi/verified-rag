Acme Widgets — Returns Policy (fictional sample document)

Customers may return any unused widget within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. The item must be in its original packaging and accompanied by the order number.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 business days of the returned item arriving at our warehouse. Shipping charges are non-refundable except where the return is the result of an error on our part.

Custom-engraved widgets are final sale and cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.
